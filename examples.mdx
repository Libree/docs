## Report Description Tips

To get the best automated reports from Pond3r, follow these description guidelines:

1. **Be specific about timeframes**: Instead of "regularly," specify "daily," "weekly," or "monthly" delivery

2. **Define clear criteria**: Specify market cap ranges, volume thresholds, or percentage changes you want to track

3. **Include risk parameters**: Mention risk tolerance levels, volatility preferences, or safety criteria

4. **Specify protocols and chains**: Mention which blockchains, DEXs, or protocols you want to monitor

5. **Define success metrics**: Clearly state what constitutes an opportunity (APY, volume growth, liquidity increases)

6. **Set delivery preferences**: Specify email delivery schedule and any alert thresholds for urgent opportunities
